## 4. Retrospective

### What worked well
- The overall UI structure came together well, with reusable components like `WideOption`, `OptionGroup`, `TripCard` and `PopUp` reducing duplication across screens.
- Navigation between all implemented screens works correctly.
- Task division between team members was clear and effective for the UI side of the sprint.

### What didn't work
- Time estimation for the itinerary screen was underestimated given its complexity.
- Too much time was spent on screens that were not strictly required for this sprint.

### What we will improve in the next sprint
- Prioritise documentation tasks earlier in the sprint rather than leaving them last.
- Improve time estimates for complex screens based on the experience from this sprint.
