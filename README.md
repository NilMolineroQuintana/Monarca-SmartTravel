# 🦋 Monarca SmartTravel

**Monarca SmartTravel** is a modern travel planning application designed to bring peace and organization to your adventures. Inspired by the migration of the Monarch butterfly, the app symbolizes freedom, summer, and the transition between destinations.

## 📖 About the Project
Monarca focuses on the "Smart" side of travel: meticulous pre-trip planning. We believe that a well-organized itinerary is the key to a peaceful journey. The name reflects the feeling of summer and the natural instinct to explore new horizons.

---

## 🔥 Key Features
- **Smart Itineraries:** Detailed organization of flights, hotels, restaurants, and locations.
- **Integrated Budgeting:** Keep track of your travel expenses at a glance for each activity.
- **Visual Countdown:** Dynamic trip cards that show exactly how many days are left until your next "migration."
- **Premium UI/UX:** High-quality visuals featuring blur effects, gradients, and custom Material 3 components.
- **Dark Mode Support:** Fully optimized for both light and dark environments.

---

## 📲 Tech Stack
- **Language:** Kotlin
- **UI Framework:** Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
- **Navigation:** Navigation Compose
- **Design:** Custom theme based on a "Magenta Rose" and "Peachy Pink" palette.
